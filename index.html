    Signal Journey: Navigating by visual and aural signals to diminish proximity

    Team (6 people), VR Development / Sep.2023-Feb.2024 2023

    This project explores the emotional effects of proximity in VR, using the interaction of cool and warm air to influence players' visual and auditory experiences. Players, equipped with VR headsets and controllers, move through four stages of distance from long-range to very close-range, experiencing intensified color saturation, sound diversity, and volume as they approach each other. Early stages involve exchanging flowers to locate the other player, while the close-range stage utilizes 3-D audio for navigation, offering a novel way to perceive emotional closeness. (video) (page)

    Team (2 people), Design & Planning / Sep.-Nov. 2023
