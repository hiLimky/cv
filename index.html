Research Interests

HCI, User Engagement and motivation in video games, Applications of AI

As a final-year undergraduate student at Sogang University, I am venturing through an academic journey that combines the fascinating realms of Art & Technology, Computer Science, and Artificial Intelligence. This multifaceted education has instilled in me a profound appreciation for Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), especially in the context of user engagement and motivation in Extended Reality (XR) environments.

To me, HCI symbolizes a remarkable crossroad where advanced technology meets user-centric experiences. Within the fast-emerging field of XR, I perceive a world rich with potential for innovative interface design principles, and I am excited to contribute my own ideas—still being refined and expanded—about how we can use game design principles and interactive technologies to create immersive and enjoyable experiences.

My experience so far, through various project work and teaching roles, has provided me with a foundation in technical skills and creative problem-solving, and I am grateful for the recognition I have received. Still, I am fully aware that I am on a lifelong learning journey, and there is so much more for me to explore and master.
